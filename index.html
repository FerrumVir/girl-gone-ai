Her Story

The cheerleader who also aced her MIT classes — then built a business with both.

I'm Jaci. Blue eyes, big dreams, and a brain that never stops. At 25, I was deep inside an AI company — surrounded by brilliant engineers, cutting-edge models, and the kind of energy that makes you feel like the future is being built right in front of you.

But I didn't want to just watch the future get built. I wanted to build it myself. I took everything I learned — the prompting, the workflows, the systems thinking — and launched Girl Gone AI. Not a side project. Not a hobby. A platform.

Think of me as the girl who can explain transformer architectures at brunch and still pick the perfect outfit for a product launch. Brains and energy. That's the whole brand.
