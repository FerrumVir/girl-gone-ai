The Story

I did lashes. Now I build digital empires.

Hey, I'm Jaci. At 25, I was doing eyelash extensions, facials, and building my esthetician clientele one appointment at a time. I loved the craft — but I wanted to build something bigger.

Then I discovered AI. Not as a buzzword, but as a real tool to create digital products at scale. Templates, planners, prompt packs, business tools — I went from booking clients in a salon chair to building a product catalog of 140+ digital downloads.

Girl Gone AI is for the people who are exactly where I was — talented, ambitious, and ready to build something that scales beyond trading time for money.
