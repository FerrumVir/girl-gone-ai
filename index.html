The Story

The girl who understood the code — and decided to write her own.

I'm Jaci. At 25, I was working at an AI company surrounded by brilliant engineers and cutting-edge tech. I loved the energy, the innovation, the feeling of being at the center of something massive.

But I didn't just want to be part of someone else's vision. I wanted to take everything I learned — the prompting, the workflows, the systems thinking — and build my own thing. So I did. I launched Girl Gone AI to create premium digital products powered by real AI expertise, not just buzzwords.

Think of it like this: the cheerleader who also aced her MIT classes — and then built a business with both the brains and the energy. That's Girl Gone AI.
