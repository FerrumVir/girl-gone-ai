# Emergency Preparedness Binder — Printable Template System

> Print this binder and store it in a physical grab-and-go location. When power is out and internet is unavailable, this binder contains every critical document reference, contact, and checklist your household needs.

---

> **SETUP GUIDE — Complete in 1-2 Hours**
>
> 1. Print all sections of this template (or copy into Google Docs and format for printing)
> 2. Get a 3-ring binder with divider tabs (8 tabs)
> 3. Create tabs: Contacts, Medical, Insurance, Financial, Evacuation, 72-Hour Kit, Pet Plan, Documents Checklist
> 4. Fill in each section with your household's actual information
> 5. Store in a known, accessible location (near exit door)
> 6. Make one digital backup copy (encrypted, in cloud storage)
> 7. Update every 6 months (set a calendar reminder for January 1 and July 1)
>
> **Tip:** Fill this out now, when you are calm and have time. In an emergency, you will not have the clarity to gather this information. Complete it once, maintain it twice a year.
