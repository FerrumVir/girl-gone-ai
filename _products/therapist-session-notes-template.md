[Broad outcome — e.g., "Client will experience a sustained reduction in depressive symptoms and improved daily functioning as evidenced by PHQ-9 score below 10 for 4 consecutive weeks."]

| # | Objective | Target Date | Measurement | Status |

## PROGRESS NOTE QUICK REFERENCE

### Common CBT Interventions (for documentation)
- Cognitive restructuring using Socratic questioning
- Behavioral experiment design and review
- Thought record completion and analysis
- Activity scheduling / behavioral activation
- Graded exposure (specify target)
- Core belief identification
- Intermediate belief / rule testing

### Common DBT Interventions
- Diary card review and chain analysis
- Distress tolerance skill: [TIPP / STOP / Radical Acceptance / Pros and Cons]
- Emotion regulation skill: [Check the Facts / Opposite Action / ABC PLEASE]
- Interpersonal effectiveness skill: [DEAR MAN / GIVE / FAST]
- Mindfulness: [Wise Mind / Observe / Describe / Participate]

### Common Trauma Interventions
- Psychoeducation on trauma responses / window of tolerance
- Grounding techniques (5-4-3-2-1, body scan, container)
- Narrative exposure / trauma processing
- EMDR: Phase [1-8], target: [description]
- Cognitive processing: stuck point identification and challenging
- Resourcing / safe place installation

### Risk Documentation Language
- **No SI:** "Client denies current suicidal ideation. No plan, intent, or means identified."
- **Passive SI:** "Client endorses passive suicidal ideation ('wish I wouldn't wake up') without plan, intent, or means. Protective factors include [list]. Safety plan reviewed."
- **Active SI with plan:** "Client reports active suicidal ideation with identified plan of [method]. Access to means assessed: [yes/no]. Safety plan [updated/created]. [Actions taken: removed access, increased frequency, emergency contact notified, hospitalization discussed]."
