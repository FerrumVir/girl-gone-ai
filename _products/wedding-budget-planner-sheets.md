## Setup Guide

### Getting Started (15 minutes)

**Step 1: Make Your Copy**
- Click "File" > "Make a copy" to save this to your own Google Drive
- Rename it: "[Your Names] Wedding Planner"

**Step 2: Set Your Budget (Tab 2)**
- Enter your total wedding budget at the top
- Fill in estimated amounts for each category based on your priorities
- Allocate 5-10% as an emergency fund — you will need it

**Step 3: Start Your Guest List (Tab 5)**
- Enter all potential guests now, even if you haven't sent invitations
- Group by household to track invitation count vs. headcount
- Mark "A-list" (definitely invited) and "B-list" (invited if space allows) in the notes column

**Step 4: Begin Vendor Research (Tab 3)**
- For each vendor category, get 3-4 quotes
- Use the comparison template to score objectively
- Book time-sensitive vendors first: venue, photographer, caterer, band/DJ

**Step 5: Set Up Your Timeline (Tab 6)**
- Count backward from your wedding date
- Adjust task due dates based on your specific timeline
- Assign tasks between both partners and any helpers

### Tips for Ongoing Use

- **Review the Dashboard (Tab 1) weekly** — it's your command center
- **Update the Payment Schedule (Tab 4)** every time you sign a contract
- **Keep the Guest List (Tab 5) current** — it feeds your caterer headcount, seating chart, and stationery count
- **Share with your partner** — both of you should have edit access at all times
- **Share view-only with parents** if they're contributing financially — transparency prevents conflicts
- **Share with your coordinator** if you have one — they can update vendor details and timelines directly
