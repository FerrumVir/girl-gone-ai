## SECTION 1: EMAIL DRAFTING

### Prompt 1: Cold Outreach Email
```
Write a cold outreach email from [YOUR NAME/COMPANY] to [PROSPECT NAME] at [PROSPECT COMPANY].

Context:
- My product/service: [DESCRIBE WHAT YOU SELL]
- Their likely pain point: [PAIN POINT YOU SOLVE]
- Desired CTA: [WHAT YOU WANT THEM TO DO — book a call, reply, visit a link]

Requirements:
- Subject line under 6 words
- Opening line that references something specific about their company
- Keep body under 100 words
- End with a low-friction ask
- Tone: [professional/casual/friendly]
```

### Prompt 2: Follow-Up Email Sequence
```
Write a 3-email follow-up sequence for a prospect who hasn't responded to my initial outreach.

Original email context: [PASTE YOUR ORIGINAL EMAIL OR SUMMARIZE]
Days between emails: [3/5/7]
Tone: [professional but persistent/friendly/direct]

For each email:
- Different angle or value proposition
- Under 75 words
- Clear subject line
- Soft CTA that doesn't feel pushy

Email 1: Reference the original email briefly
Email 2: Share a relevant result or case study
Email 3: Breakup email giving them an easy out
```

## BONUS: PROMPT CHAINS (Multi-Step Workflows)

### Chain 1: Meeting to Action (3-prompt sequence)
```
Step 1 — Paste after meeting: "Here are my raw meeting notes. Extract all decisions, action items with owners, and open questions: [PASTE NOTES]"

Step 2 — Follow immediately: "Now write a follow-up email to all attendees ([LIST NAMES]) summarizing the decisions and action items. Keep it under 150 words."

Step 3 — Follow immediately: "Create a simple tracking table for the action items with columns: Task, Owner, Due Date, Status. Format as markdown."
```

### Chain 2: Content Repurposing (4-prompt sequence)
```
Step 1: "Take this blog post and extract the 5 most shareable insights: [PASTE BLOG POST]"

Step 2: "Turn each insight into a LinkedIn post (under 200 words each, hook in first line, end with a question)"

Step 3: "Now turn the same 5 insights into Twitter/X threads (5 tweets each, punchy and direct)"

Step 4: "Finally, create 5 Instagram carousel outlines (slide-by-slide text for each insight)"
```

### Chain 3: Customer Research to Copy (3-prompt sequence)
```
Step 1: "Analyze these customer reviews and extract the top 5 pain points, top 5 desires, and the exact language they use: [PASTE 10-20 REVIEWS]"

Step 2: "Using the voice-of-customer language from above, write 5 headline variations for a landing page selling [PRODUCT]"

Step 3: "Now write the full landing page copy using those headlines and customer language. Structure: Headline → Problem → Solution → Benefits → Social proof → CTA"
```
