# TikTok Content Strategy Planner
## Content Calendars, Trending Sounds, Posting Schedule, Analytics & Content Pillars

---

> **HOW TO USE THIS PLANNER**
>
> This digital planner is built for TikTok creators, brands, and social media managers who want to grow strategically (not randomly). Whether you are posting 1x/day or 5x/day, this system keeps you organized and intentional.
>
> 1. Define your Content Pillars first — these guide every piece of content
> 2. Set up your Posting Schedule for consistency
> 3. Use the Content Calendar to batch-plan 1-2 weeks ahead
> 4. Track trending sounds and hashtags in the Trends section
> 5. Review your Analytics weekly to double down on what works
>
> **Tip:** The TikTok algorithm rewards consistency and watch time. Post at least once daily, hook viewers in the first 1-2 seconds, and keep videos short enough that people rewatch. This planner helps you build systems around those principles.

# SECTION 6: HOOK FORMULAS & SCRIPTS

---

## Proven Hook Templates

| Hook Type | Template | Example | Best For |
|-----------|----------|---------|----------|
| Curiosity Gap | "Nobody talks about [X]..." | "Nobody talks about the dark side of working from home" | Hot takes, education |
| Direct Address | "If you're a [type], you need this" | "If you're a new plant mom, you need this hack" | Targeted content |
| Controversy | "Unpopular opinion: [statement]" | "Unpopular opinion: morning routines are overrated" | Engagement bait |
| Story | "The time I [dramatic event]..." | "The time I got fired and made more money" | Storytelling |
| Tutorial | "How to [result] in [timeframe]" | "How to edit TikToks like a pro in 5 minutes" | Educational |
| List | "[Number] things that [result]" | "5 things that changed my skin forever" | Saves, watch time |
| Before/After | "Watch this transformation..." | "Watch this $5 thrift flip transformation" | Visual content |
| Question | "Why does nobody talk about [X]?" | "Why does nobody talk about how expensive healthy eating is?" | Commentary |
| Fear of Missing Out | "Stop scrolling if you [characteristic]" | "Stop scrolling if you have textured skin" | Targeted reach |
| Social Proof | "[Number] people asked me about this" | "100 people asked me about my skincare routine" | Authority |
