# Daily Glow Planner
### Your 5-Minute Morning Ritual + 2-Minute Evening Reflection

---

> **HOW TO USE THIS PLANNER**
>
> Morning: Fill out the Morning Page (5 minutes max).
> Throughout the day: Check off your priorities as you complete them.
> Evening: Fill out the Evening Reflection (2 minutes max).
> Weekly: Complete the Weekly Reflection on Sunday.
>
> That's it. Simple by design. Consistency over complexity.
