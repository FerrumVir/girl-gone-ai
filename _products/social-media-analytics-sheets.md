# Social Media Analytics Dashboard

Welcome to your Social Media Analytics Dashboard. This Google Sheets template tracks performance across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Twitter/X, and LinkedIn — giving you engagement rates, posting time insights, follower projections, and professional monthly reports in one place.

---

## Setup Guide

### Step 1: Make Your Copy
1. Click **File > Make a copy** to save this template to your Google Drive.
2. Rename your copy (e.g., "Social Media Analytics — [Your Name/Brand] 2026").

### Step 2: Customize Your Platforms
- Navigate to each platform-specific tab and update the profile information fields at the top (handle, current follower count, account type).
- If you don't use a particular platform, you can hide that tab (right-click the tab > Hide sheet).

### Step 3: Enter Your Data
- Start logging posts in the **Content Tracker** tab.
- Enter follower counts in the **Follower Growth** tab at least weekly (daily is ideal).
- The Dashboard, Engagement Calculator, Posting Time Analyzer, Content Scorer, and Report tabs update automatically.

### Step 4: Review Sample Data
- Sample data is pre-loaded in gray/italic text. Review it to understand how each field works, then delete it and start entering your own.

## Tips for Getting the Most Out of This Dashboard

1. **Log consistently.** The system only works if you feed it data. Set a daily reminder to log your posts — it takes 30 seconds per entry.
2. **Enter at least 2-3 weeks of data** before drawing conclusions from the posting time analyzer or content scorer.
3. **Review the monthly report** on the 1st of each month. Use it to plan your content strategy for the coming month.
4. **Focus on engagement rate, not vanity metrics.** A post with 500 impressions and a 10% engagement rate is more valuable than one with 50,000 impressions and a 0.1% engagement rate.
5. **Use the Performance Scorer to find your formula.** Look for patterns in your top-scoring content: what type, what topic, what time, what platform.
6. **Customize the scoring weights** to match your goals. If your priority is website traffic, increase the weight on Clicks. If it's brand awareness, increase the weight on Shares.
