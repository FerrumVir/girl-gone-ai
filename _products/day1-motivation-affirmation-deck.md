# Motivation Affirmation Deck
### 100 Affirmations Across 5 Categories -- Print, Save, Use Daily

---

> **HOW TO USE THIS DECK**
>
> 1. Print these pages and cut along the lines to create cards (business card size, 10 per page)
> 2. Or save the full list and read one each morning
> 3. Pick a category that matches what you need right now
> 4. Read the affirmation out loud, slowly, once
> 5. Then read it again like you mean it
>
> These are not wishes. They are declarations. Say them even when they feel untrue -- especially when they feel untrue. That is when they work hardest.

# HOW TO USE THESE DAILY

---

## Option 1: Morning Card Pull

Shuffle your printed cards (or scroll with your eyes closed and stop randomly). Read the card you land on. That is your affirmation for the day. Write it on a sticky note and put it where you will see it.

## Option 2: Category Focus Week

Spend one week per category. Read all 20 affirmations in that category on Day 1, then pick the 3 that hit hardest. Focus on those 3 for the rest of the week.

## Option 3: Emergency Interrupt

When you feel anxiety, self-doubt, or a comparison spiral starting, pull out this deck (or open this file). Read 3 affirmations from the category that matches what you are feeling. It takes 30 seconds and it interrupts the thought pattern.

## Option 4: Journaling Prompts

Pick any card and use it as a journal prompt. Write for 5 minutes about:

- Why does this affirmation feel true or untrue for me right now?
- When was the last time I needed to hear this?
- What would change if I fully believed this?
