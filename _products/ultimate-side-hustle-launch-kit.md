# SECTION 1: IDEA VALIDATION SCORECARD

---

## The Idea

**My side hustle idea in one sentence:**
```
I will help [WHO] do/get [WHAT] by [HOW], charged as [PRICING MODEL].
```

**Fill in:**
- WHO: [TARGET CUSTOMER]
- WHAT: [OUTCOME THEY GET]
- HOW: [YOUR METHOD/PRODUCT]
- PRICING: [PER ITEM / MONTHLY / HOURLY / ETC.]

---

## Validation Scoring Matrix

Rate each dimension 1-5. A score of 20+ means GO. Under 15, rethink the idea.

| Dimension | Question | Score (1-5) | Evidence/Notes |
|-----------|----------|-------------|----------------|
| **Demand** | Are people actively searching for this or paying for alternatives? | [1-5] | [EVIDENCE] |
| **Willingness to Pay** | Would your target customer pay real money (not just say "cool idea")? | [1-5] | [EVIDENCE] |
| **Competition** | Is the market not too crowded AND not empty? (Both extremes are bad) | [1-5] | [EVIDENCE] |
| **Distribution** | Do you know exactly WHERE to find your first 10 customers? | [1-5] | [EVIDENCE] |
| **Unfair Advantage** | Do you have skills, experience, or access that others don't? | [1-5] | [EVIDENCE] |
| **Time to Revenue** | Can you make your first dollar within 30 days? | [1-5] | [EVIDENCE] |

**TOTAL SCORE:** ___/30

**Verdict:**
- 25-30: Strong idea. Move fast.
- 20-24: Good idea. Shore up weak dimensions.
- 15-19: Needs work. Which dimensions are dragging?
- Under 15: Pivot. Something fundamental isn't there.

- [ ] Posted about the idea and got genuine interest (not just "nice idea!")
- [ ] Found evidence people are PAYING for alternatives already

## Outreach Templates

### Template 1: Warm Outreach (people you know)

```
Hey [NAME],

I'm building [WHAT] for [WHO] and I immediately thought of you.

It helps with [SPECIFIC PROBLEM] by [HOW]. I'm offering it at
[LAUNCH PRICE — usually discounted] for early supporters.

Would this be useful for you? I'd also love any feedback since
you're exactly who I built it for.

[LINK or "Want me to send details?"]
```

### Template 2: Cold Outreach (people you don't know)

```
Hi [NAME],

I noticed you [WHAT THEY DO/POSTED ABOUT] and wanted to reach out.

I just created [PRODUCT/SERVICE] that helps [TARGET AUDIENCE] with
[SPECIFIC PROBLEM]. I think it might be relevant to you because
[SPECIFIC REASON].

Would you be open to checking it out? Happy to answer any questions.

[YOUR NAME]
```

- [BENEFIT 3]

to give feedback in exchange for [DISCOUNT/BONUS].

## Time Block Template

Assign your limited hours to these categories:

| Category | Hours/Week | When (Specific Blocks) | This Week's Tasks |
|----------|-----------|------------------------|-------------------|
| BUILD (creating product/service) | [#] hrs | [DAYS/TIMES] | [TASKS] |
| SELL (outreach, marketing, sales) | [#] hrs | [DAYS/TIMES] | [TASKS] |
| ADMIN (setup, systems, email) | [#] hrs | [DAYS/TIMES] | [TASKS] |
| LEARN (research, skills) | [#] hrs | [DAYS/TIMES] | [TASKS] |

**Rules:**
- Minimum 50% of time on BUILD or SELL (the only two that make money)
- Maximum 20% on ADMIN
- No more than 10% on LEARN (avoid "research mode" procrastination)
- Protect your blocks like meetings — they're non-negotiable

---

## Burnout Prevention Checklist

- [ ] I have at least 1 full day off per week (no side hustle work)
- [ ] I'm sleeping 7+ hours most nights
- [ ] I have a clear "shutdown" time for side hustle work each day
- [ ] I'm not sacrificing relationships or health for this
- [ ] I have a timeline for when I'll reassess (not "forever")
- [ ] I know my "quit conditions" — what would make me stop
