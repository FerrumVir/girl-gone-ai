## SECTION 1: PROFILE OPTIMIZATION

### Headline Formula (220 characters max)
Your headline shows up everywhere — search results, comments, DMs. Don't waste it on just your job title.

**Formula:** [ROLE] | Helping [WHO] do [WHAT] | [PROOF/HOOK]

**Templates:**
```
Template A: [Role] at [Company] | I write about [TOPIC] for [AUDIENCE]
Example: Product Manager at Stripe | I write about career growth for tech professionals who hate networking

Template B: [OUTCOME you deliver] for [WHO] | [CREDENTIAL]
Example: Turning founders into thought leaders | Ghostwriter for 12 CEOs with 100K+ followers

Template C: [Role] | [TOPIC 1] + [TOPIC 2] + [TOPIC 3] | [CTA]
Example: Marketing Director | Brand strategy + content systems + team building | Follow for weekly frameworks

Template D: [Role] helping [AUDIENCE] go from [STATE A] to [STATE B]
Example: Career coach helping mid-career professionals go from overlooked to sought-after
```

### About Section Template (2,600 characters max)
```
[OPENING HOOK — a bold statement, question, or mini-story that stops the scroll]

Here's what I believe: [YOUR CORE PHILOSOPHY ABOUT YOUR WORK IN 1-2 SENTENCES]

I help [WHO] achieve [WHAT OUTCOME] by [HOW YOU DO IT].

[OPTIONAL: Brief origin story — 2-3 sentences about how you got here]

What I'm known for:
- [EXPERTISE AREA 1]
- [EXPERTISE AREA 2]
- [EXPERTISE AREA 3]

[SOCIAL PROOF — metrics, clients, publications, awards — pick 2-3]

Currently:
- [WHAT YOU'RE WORKING ON / AVAILABLE FOR]
- [WHERE PEOPLE CAN FIND MORE FROM YOU]

[CTA — what should someone do after reading this?]
Best way to reach me: [EMAIL OR DM INSTRUCTION]
```

### Profile Optimization Checklist
```
- [ ] Profile photo: Professional, clear face, consistent with your brand (warm smile > serious face for most niches)
- [ ] Banner image: Custom graphic with your value proposition or content themes
- [ ] Headline: Uses the formula above (not just "Job Title at Company")
- [ ] About section: Written in first person, tells a story, includes CTA
- [ ] Featured section: Pin your best 3 posts, a lead magnet link, or your website
- [ ] Experience: Written as accomplishments (not job descriptions) with metrics
- [ ] Skills: Top 3 skills pinned are your primary keywords
- [ ] Recommendations: At least 3 recent ones (ask for them specifically)
- [ ] Creator mode: ON (shows "Follow" instead of "Connect" — better for growth)
- [ ] Custom URL: linkedin.com/in/yourname (clean, no random numbers)
- [ ] Activity visible: Your recent posts show on your profile
```

## SECTION 3: COMMENTING STRATEGY

### The 10-5-2 Method (Daily, 15 min)
```
1. Comment on 10 posts from people in your niche (mix of your size and bigger)
2. Reply to 5 comments on YOUR posts (keep conversations going)
3. Send 2 thoughtful DMs to people whose content resonated

Rules for comments:
- Minimum 10 words (more = better)
- Add value: share your experience, ask a smart question, or expand on a point
- First line should hook (it shows in notifications)
- Comment within 1 hour of the post going live (algorithm rewards early engagement)
```

### Comment Templates That Get Noticed
```
TEMPLATE A (Share Experience):
"This reminds me of when I [SIMILAR EXPERIENCE]. The thing that made the difference was [YOUR INSIGHT]. Thanks for articulating it better than I could."

TEMPLATE B (Ask a Smart Question):
"Great point about [SPECIFIC THING]. I'm curious — how do you handle [RELATED SCENARIO THAT'S SLIGHTLY DIFFERENT]? I've been experimenting with [YOUR APPROACH] but would love your take."

TEMPLATE C (Expand on the Point):
"Adding to this: I've found that [RELATED INSIGHT THEY DIDN'T MENTION]. Especially true when [CONTEXT]. This post + that = the full picture."

TEMPLATE D (Disagree Respectfully):
"Interesting take. I've actually seen [ALTERNATIVE SCENARIO] play out differently — [BRIEF EXPLANATION]. Could be industry-specific though. Curious if others have seen this too."

TEMPLATE E (Validate + Amplify):
"I needed to hear this today. Currently dealing with [SITUATION] and your point about [SPECIFIC THING] just reframed how I'm approaching it. Thank you."
```

## SECTION 5: CONTENT REPURPOSING SYSTEM

### One Post, Five Formats
Take your best-performing LinkedIn post and turn it into:

```
1. TWITTER/X THREAD: Break into 5-8 tweets. First tweet = hook + promise. Each subsequent tweet = one key point. Last tweet = CTA + retweet request.

2. INSTAGRAM CAROUSEL: Each key point becomes one slide. Slide 1 = hook/title. Last slide = CTA. Keep text large and scannable.

3. EMAIL NEWSLETTER: Expand the post into a 500-word email. Add personal context, one extra example, and a reader-specific CTA.

4. SHORT VIDEO (REEL/TikTok): You talking to camera delivering the key points. Use the original hook as your opening line. Keep under 60 seconds.

5. BLOG POST: Expand into a 1000-1500 word article. Add research, examples, subheadings, and SEO keywords. Embed the original LinkedIn post as social proof.
```

### Content Calendar Framework (Weekly)
```
Monday: Story/personal experience post (high engagement day)
Tuesday: Framework/how-to post (educational, shareable)
Wednesday: Industry take or opinion (conversation starter)
Thursday: Carousel or list post (saves + shares)
Friday: Engagement post — poll, question, or fill-in-the-blank
Saturday/Sunday: Repurpose a top performer from the week before (or rest)
```

## SECTION 6: GROWTH METRICS & BENCHMARKS

### Track Weekly
```
- Impressions (total views on all posts)
- Engagement rate per post: (reactions + comments + shares) / impressions
- Profile views
- Connection requests received
- Follower growth
- Top-performing post (and what format/topic it was)
- Inbound DMs or leads
```

### Benchmarks
```
- Good engagement rate: 3-5% of impressions
- Great engagement rate: 5-10%
- Viral threshold: post reaches 10x your follower count in impressions
- Healthy weekly growth: 50-200 net new followers (varies by niche)
- Target comments-to-likes ratio: 1:10 or better (comments matter more than likes)
```

### Optimization Rules
```
- Post that got 2x your average? → Make more like that format/topic
- Post that flopped? → Don't repeat that format/topic combo for 2 weeks
- Comments died after you posted? → Try a different time slot next week
- Getting saves but no comments? → Add a question at the end
- Getting comments but no shares? → Make the content more "frameworky" (shareable = makes THEM look smart)
```
