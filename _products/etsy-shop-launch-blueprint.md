# SECTION 1: SHOP SETUP MASTER CHECKLIST (50+ Items)

---

## Account & Legal Setup

- [ ] Create Etsy account (or convert personal to seller)
- [ ] Choose shop name (check availability + domain + social handles)
- [ ] Set up Etsy Payments (link bank account)
- [ ] Set up billing for Etsy fees
- [ ] Add taxpayer information
- [ ] Determine business structure (sole proprietor, LLC, etc.)
- [ ] Check state/local business license requirements
- [ ] Set up separate business bank account (recommended)
- [ ] Record EIN or SSN for tax reporting
- [ ] Understand Etsy fee structure:
  - Listing fee: $0.20 per listing (renews every 4 months or at sale)
  - Transaction fee: 6.5% of sale price + shipping
  - Payment processing: 3% + $0.25
  - Offsite ads fee: 15% (if applicable, shops under $10K can opt out)

# SECTION 6: REVIEW GENERATION STRATEGY

---

## Getting Your First 10 Reviews

Reviews are everything on Etsy. Listings with 5+ reviews convert significantly better.

### Method 1: Friends & Family Launch

```
Message template:

"Hey [NAME]! I just launched my Etsy shop and I'm trying to get my first
few reviews. Would you be willing to purchase [product] (I'll reimburse you)?
An honest review would mean the world to me.

Here's the link: [LINK]

No pressure at all — just thought I'd ask!"
```

**Note:** Etsy prohibits fake reviews. Only ask people who will genuinely use and honestly review the product. Never offer incentives for positive reviews specifically.

### Method 2: Post-Purchase Follow-Up

```
Send via Etsy messages, 5-7 days after purchase:

"Hi [BUYER NAME]!

I just wanted to check in and make sure you received your download okay
and everything is working perfectly. If you have any questions about
using [product name], I'm happy to help!

If you're enjoying it, I'd be so grateful if you'd leave a quick review
on Etsy — it really helps small shops like mine get found by other
customers. Either way, thank you so much for your support!

[YOUR NAME]"
```

### Method 3: Insert a Thank-You Card (Digital)

Include a bonus page in your digital download:

```
THANK YOU!

Thank you for supporting a small business. I hope you love this [product].

If you have a moment, leaving a review on Etsy helps other
customers find products like this one.

Need help? Have feedback? Reach me at [ETSY SHOP MESSAGES].

Enjoy!
— [YOUR NAME]
```
