# Glow-Up Transformation Tracker
### 90 Days of Intentional Change Across Skincare, Fitness, Style & Mindset

---

> **HOW TO USE THIS TRACKER**
>
> This is a 90-day transformation system. It tracks FOUR areas simultaneously:
> Skincare, Fitness, Style, and Mindset.
>
> 1. Set your 90-day goals (Section 1)
> 2. Use the daily tracking page every day (Section 2)
> 3. Do your weekly photo log + check-in (Section 3)
> 4. Take monthly measurements and review progress (Section 4)
> 5. Build habits that stick using the stacking system (Section 5)
>
> This isn't about perfection. It's about showing up for yourself consistently.

# SECTION 5: HABIT STACKING SYSTEM

---

## How Habit Stacking Works

Attach new habits to existing ones. Format: "After I [CURRENT HABIT], I will [NEW HABIT]."

## My Habit Stacks

### Morning Stack

```
After I wake up, I will...
  → Drink a full glass of water
  → Then do 5 minutes of stretching
  → Then apply my AM skincare routine
  → Then take my supplements
  → Then do 3 minutes of meditation/affirmation
```

### Post-Workout Stack

```
After I finish my workout, I will...
  → Drink a protein-rich drink/snack
  → Then take a progress photo (if photo day)
  → Then log my workout in the tracker
```

### Evening Stack

```
After I finish dinner, I will...
  → Put my phone in another room
  → Then do my PM skincare routine
  → Then journal for 5 minutes (gratitude + reflection)
  → Then lay out tomorrow's outfit
  → Then read for 15 minutes
```

### Style Stack (Weekly)

```
Every Sunday evening, I will...
  → Review next week's schedule
  → Then plan 5 outfits for the week
  → Then check if any items need washing/prep
  → Then lay out Monday's outfit
```
