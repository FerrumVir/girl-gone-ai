# Beauty TikTok Content Prompts — 50+ Video Ideas & Scripts

> Copy-paste video concepts organized by category. Each includes a hook, script outline, and format notes. Adapt to your specific niche (lash, skin, hair, nails, brows, makeup).

---

## CATEGORY 1: TRANSFORMATIONS (10 Ideas)

### 1. The Silent Reveal

**Hook:** "No talking. Just watch." (text on screen)
**Format:** Process video, trending audio, 15-30s
**Script/Flow:**
- Open on the "before" (1-2 seconds)
- Quick cuts of the process (hands working, products, tools)
- Build tension with the music
- Dramatic reveal at the beat drop
- Final shot: client seeing themselves
**Notes:** Keep clips 1-2 seconds each. Use a satisfying sound for the reveal moment.

### 2. The Side-by-Side Split Screen

**Hook:** "The left side is done. Watch me finish the right."
**Format:** Split screen or half-face, 30-45s
**Script/Flow:**
- Show one side complete
- Film yourself doing the other side in real-time (sped up)
- Reveal the full finished result
- End with client reaction
**Notes:** Works amazing for lashes, brows, makeup. Duet format also works.

### 3. The Time-Lapse Transformation

**Hook:** "2 hours in 30 seconds"
**Format:** Overhead or front-facing time-lapse, 15-30s
**Script/Flow:**
- Set up phone on tripod/mount before starting
- Record entire service
- Speed up to 15-30 seconds
- Add satisfying music
- Before/after comparison at end
**Notes:** Fixed camera angle is key. Clean workspace makes it more satisfying.

### 4. The "She Hasn't Had [Service] in X Months"

**Hook:** "She hasn't had her [lashes/facial/brows] done in 6 months. Watch this."
**Format:** 30-45s, voiceover or text
**Script/Flow:**
- Close-up of the "before" state (zoomed in on the problem)
- Text: "6 months with no maintenance"
- Quick process clips
- Dramatic after reveal
- Text: "Welcome back, queen"
**Notes:** The longer the gap, the more dramatic the content. Great for retention fills, overgrown brows, etc.

### 5. The Expectation vs. Reality

**Hook:** "She showed me this inspo pic..."
**Format:** 30-45s
**Script/Flow:**
- Show the inspiration photo (screenshot on phone)
- Show the starting point (the before)
- Quick process clips
- Reveal the result
- Side-by-side with the inspo pic
- Text: "Expectation met" or "I think we nailed it"
**Notes:** Pick inspo photos that are achievable. Underpromise, overdeliver on camera.

### 6. The Dramatic Zoom-Out

**Hook:** (Start super zoomed in on detail work)
**Format:** 15-20s, trending audio with build-up
**Script/Flow:**
- Open extremely close-up (texture, detail, individual lashes, pores, etc.)
- Slowly zoom out as music builds
- Reveal full face/result at the peak of the song
**Notes:** The zoom itself creates anticipation. Use smooth zoom, not jumpy.

### 7. "1st Appointment vs. 5th Appointment"

**Hook:** "How it started vs. how it's going"
**Format:** 15-30s, side-by-side or sequential
**Script/Flow:**
- Show their very first result with you
- Show their most recent result
- Text: "Trust the process"
- Show the progression (if you have photos from each visit)
**Notes:** Shows your skill growth AND the benefit of consistency. Great for series treatments.

### 8. The Client Reaction

**Hook:** "I live for this moment"
**Format:** 30s, authentic audio
**Script/Flow:**
- Quick clips of you working
- The moment you hand them the mirror
- Their genuine reaction (gasps, smiles, tears)
- Freeze frame on their smile
**Notes:** MUST be authentic. Don't coach the reaction. Have your camera ready before the mirror moment.

### 9. "POV: You Finally Booked"

**Hook:** "POV: You finally booked the appointment you've been thinking about for 3 months"
**Format:** 20-30s, client POV angle
**Script/Flow:**
- Film from the client's perspective (looking up at you working)
- Show the calming environment
- Skip to the reveal (from their POV in the mirror)
- Text: "Why did I wait so long?"
**Notes:** Makes viewers imagine themselves as the client. Aspirational.

### 10. The "You vs. the [Service] You Did at Home"

**Hook:** "At-home version vs. professional version"
**Format:** 30-45s, comparison
**Script/Flow:**
- Show a common at-home attempt (DIY lash strip, at-home peel, self-wax gone wrong)
- Transition: "Now let me show you what a professional can do"
- Your result
- Side by side
**Notes:** Don't mock the person — be lighthearted. The point is "professionals exist for a reason."

## CATEGORY 2: EDUCATIONAL (10 Ideas)

### 11. "Stop Doing This"

**Hook:** "If you're still [common mistake], we need to talk."
**Format:** 30-60s, talking head or voiceover + visuals
**Script:**
- Name the mistake clearly (show it if possible)
- Explain WHY it's a problem (1-2 sentences)
- Show what to do INSTEAD
- End: "You're welcome. Save this."
**Topics:** Wrong order of skincare, sleeping on fresh lashes, picking at peeling skin, using expired products.

### 12. "The Difference Between [A] and [B]"

**Hook:** "[Service A] vs. [Service B] — and which one is right for you"
**Format:** 45-60s, split visuals or side-by-side demos
**Script:**
- Name both options
- Show what each looks like
- Explain who each is best for
- Price/time comparison
- "Comment which one you'd pick"
**Topics:** Classic vs. volume lashes. Chemical peel vs. microneedling. Gel vs. acrylic.

### 13. "What Your [Problem] Is Trying to Tell You"

**Hook:** "Your skin is literally screaming at you and you're ignoring it."
**Format:** 30-45s, educational graphics or pointing to face
**Script:**
- Name the symptom (breakouts on chin, dry patches, etc.)
- Explain what it usually means
- Give 1-2 actionable solutions
- "See a professional if it persists"
**Topics:** Face mapping for acne, what peeling means, why lashes fall out in clumps.

### 14. "Red Flags in a [Service Provider]"

**Hook:** "Run if your [esthetician/lash tech/stylist] does this..."
**Format:** 30-45s, list format with text overlay
**Script:**
- List 4-5 red flags (one per clip)
- Examples: no sanitation between clients, reusing disposables, no intake form, rushing through service, pressuring product sales
- End: "You deserve better. Green flags in part 2?"
**Notes:** Position yourself as the safe, professional alternative without being catty.

### 15. "Aftercare 101"

**Hook:** "Your results are only as good as your aftercare. Here's what to do."
**Format:** 45-60s, numbered list
**Script:**
- List 5-6 aftercare steps specific to a service
- Show what TO do (brief demos)
- Mention what NOT to do
- "Save this and follow it. Your [service] will thank you."
**Notes:** This gets saved constantly. High save rate = algorithm push.

### 16. "I'm Going to Save You $[Amount]"

**Hook:** "This tip is going to save you $200 a year on [products/services]."
**Format:** 30-45s, direct to camera
**Script:**
- Name the money-saving tip
- Explain how/why it works
- Show proof if possible
- "You're welcome. Follow for more."
**Topics:** How to make services last longer, drugstore dupes for professional products, at-home maintenance between visits.

### 17. "What Actually Happens During [Service]"

**Hook:** "Nobody shows you what actually happens during a [facial/lash appointment]. Let me fix that."
**Format:** 60-90s, real process with narration
**Script:**
- Walk through each step of the service
- Explain what you're doing and why
- Show the tools/products used
- Demystify the process
- End: "And that's it. Not scary at all."
**Notes:** Removes the fear barrier for people who've never tried the service.

### 18. "Products I'd Never Use on My Own Face"

**Hook:** "As an esthetician/beauty pro, these are products I'd NEVER put on my own skin."
**Format:** 30-45s, showing products (hide brand names or use generic descriptions)
**Script:**
- Category 1: [why]
- Category 2: [why]
- Category 3: [why]
- "Here's what I use instead" (flash your actual products)
**Notes:** Don't brand-bash. Say "products WITH [ingredient]" not specific brand names.

### 19. "1 Minute Skin/Beauty Education"

**Hook:** "Let me explain [concept] in under 60 seconds."
**Format:** Exactly 60 seconds, fast-paced
**Script:**
- Rapid-fire explanation of one concept
- Use visuals, diagrams, or demos
- End at exactly 60 seconds
- "Follow for more 60-second beauty school"
**Topics:** What retinol does, how lash cycles work, why skin purges, what SPF numbers mean.

### 20. "The Correct Order of [Routine/Steps]"

**Hook:** "Most people do this in the WRONG order. Here's the correct way."
**Format:** 30-45s, numbered visual steps
**Script:**
- Show the wrong order (X over it)
- Show the correct order step by step
- Brief reason why order matters
- "Share with someone who needs this"
**Topics:** Skincare layering, how to prep for a lash appointment, pre-service routine.

## CATEGORY 3: DAY IN THE LIFE / BEHIND THE SCENES (10 Ideas)

### 21. Full Work Day Montage

**Hook:** "5 clients. 9 hours. This is what a full day looks like."
**Format:** 60-90s, montage with music
**Flow:** Morning routine > drive/commute > setup > client 1 (brief) > transition > client 2 > quick lunch > client 3-5 > cleanup > results > heading home.

### 22. "Things I Do Between Every Client"

**Hook:** "The 15 minutes between clients that you never see"
**Format:** 30-45s, real-time or slightly sped up
**Flow:** Changing linens > sanitizing > restocking > washing hands > checking next client's notes > breathing for 30 seconds > greeting next client.

### 23. Studio/Space Tour

**Hook:** "Tour of my [studio/suite/space] where the magic happens"
**Format:** 30-60s, smooth walking tour
**Flow:** Walk through each area > point out details > show organization systems > favorite spots > "Would you get a service here? Yes or no?"

### 24. "What I Packed for a Mobile Appointment"

**Hook:** "Everything that comes with me to a mobile [service] appointment"
**Format:** 30-45s, packing/unpacking shots
**Flow:** Lay out everything > pack it up > show the setup at the client's location > the result.

### 25. Product/Supply Restock

**Hook:** "Restock and organize with me" (ASMR-style)
**Format:** 30-60s, satisfying close-up shots
**Flow:** Unboxing > organizing by category > putting everything in its place > final organized shot.

### 26. "How I Prep for a Full Day"

**Hook:** "The morning routine that makes my workday smooth"
**Format:** 45-60s
**Flow:** Wake up > personal routine > outfit > drive > studio setup > first client arrives > "Let's go."

### 27. "Closing Up Shop" Routine

**Hook:** "End of day routine (it's more than just locking the door)"
**Format:** 30-45s, calming music
**Flow:** Last client leaves > deep clean > sanitize everything > restock for tomorrow > turn off lights > lock up > drive home.

### 28. "I Spent $[X] on Supplies This Month"

**Hook:** "Transparency time: here's what it costs to run my beauty business"
**Format:** 45-60s, showing receipts/purchases
**Flow:** Show what you bought > categorize (products, disposables, equipment) > total it up > "This is why we charge what we charge."

### 29. "What Clients Don't See"

**Hook:** "Before you sit in my chair, here's what already happened"
**Format:** 30-45s
**Flow:** Education/training footage > practice on mannequins > studying new techniques > continuing education class > "The work behind the work."

### 30. Difficult Day Real Talk

**Hook:** "Not every day is perfect. Here's an honest one."
**Format:** 45-60s, talking head
**Flow:** Acknowledge a tough day (cancellation, difficult client, self-doubt) > how you handled it > what you learned > "Still showing up tomorrow."
**Notes:** Vulnerability builds connection. Don't overshare — keep it professional.

### 39. "My Hands After a Full Day of [Service]"

## CATEGORY 5: TRENDING FORMATS (10 Ideas)

### 41. "Get Ready With Me" (GRWM)

**Hook:** "GRWM while I tell you about [interesting story/tip/opinion]"
**Format:** 60-90s, your own routine
**Flow:** Do your personal beauty routine while narrating a story, tip, or opinion. The routine is visual; the narration is the real content.

### 42. "Ranking [Category] from Worst to Best"

**Hook:** "Ranking every [product type/technique/trend] I've tried"
**Format:** 30-60s, tier list or numbered ranking
**Flow:** Show each item > give quick verdict > rank them > "Agree? Fight me in the comments."

### 43. "If [Celebrity/Character] Were My Client"

**Hook:** "If [person] sat in my chair, here's what I'd do"
**Format:** 30-45s, creative/fun
**Flow:** Show a celebrity photo > analyze what they'd need > describe the service plan > mock-up the result (optional).

### 44. "3 Products Under $[Price]"

**Hook:** "3 products under $30 that outperform everything expensive"
**Format:** 30s, showing each product
**Flow:** Product 1 + what it does > Product 2 + what it does > Product 3 + what it does > "Save this."

### 45. "Photo vs. Real Life" (Honest Comparison)

**Hook:** "What my work looks like in photos vs. how it actually looks"
**Format:** 15-20s, comparison
**Flow:** Show the Instagram version > show the same work in different lighting/angles. Point: "It looks this good in PERSON too."

### 46. "Replying to Comments" (Stitch/Response)

**Hook:** "Someone asked [question] so let me answer..."
**Format:** 30-60s, reply to a comment or DM
**Flow:** Show the question > give a thorough, helpful answer > demonstrate if possible > "More questions? Drop them below."

### 47. "Monthly Favorites" / "What I Used Up"

**Hook:** "Products I used up / repurchased this month"
**Format:** 45-60s, showing empties
**Flow:** Each product > would you repurchase? > replacement pick > "Follow for next month's."

### 48. "This Sound but Make It [Your Niche]"

**Hook:** (Use any trending sound, apply it to beauty)
**Format:** Matches the trend length
**Flow:** Whatever the trend format is — adapt it to your specific niche. Examples: dramatic reveals at beat drops, outfit transitions become before/afters, storytimes become client stories.

### 49. "Things That Just Make Sense"

**Hook:** "[Your niche] things that just make sense"
**Format:** 15-30s, satisfying clips
**Flow:** Collection of satisfying moments from your work — peeling, application, reveals, organization. Set to a "things that make sense" trending audio.

### 50. "Tell Me [Your Job] Without Telling Me [Your Job]"

**Hook:** (Show visual clues without saying what you do)
**Format:** 15-20s, trending audio
**Flow:** Show: your tools, your hands, a close-up of your work, your schedule, your product shelf. Let them guess in comments.

## FILMING TIPS

### Quick Setup Checklist
- [ ] Ring light ON (or natural window light)
- [ ] Phone at eye level or overhead (not below)
- [ ] Background clean and on-brand
- [ ] Audio check (quiet room, or use trending audio)
- [ ] Camera lens cleaned
- [ ] Film in 9:16 vertical ratio
- [ ] Record 20-30% more than you need (edit down)

### Hook Rules (First 3 Seconds)
1. Start mid-action (don't start with "hey guys")
2. Use text on screen immediately
3. Create curiosity or tension
4. Pattern interrupt (unexpected visual or statement)
5. Promise a result ("Watch until the end")

### Algorithm Tips
- Post when YOUR audience is online (check analytics)
- Reply to every comment in the first hour
- Use 3-5 hashtags (not 30)
- Trending audio boosts reach but original audio builds brand
- Videos that get rewatched rank higher (keep them short and satisfying)
- Save-worthy content > like-worthy content
- Consistency beats virality. 5 posts/week > 1 viral video/month.
