# Thesis & Research Writing Planner
## Chapter Outline Tracker, Citation Manager, Advisor Meeting Log & More

---

> **HOW TO USE THIS PLANNER**
>
> This digital planner is designed for graduate students, doctoral candidates, and academic researchers working on theses, dissertations, or long-form research projects. Use it as a Google Doc, Notion template, or print sections as needed.
>
> 1. Start with the Project Overview to define your scope and timeline
> 2. Break your thesis into chapters using the Chapter Outline Tracker
> 3. Use the Citation Manager to organize sources as you research
> 4. Log all advisor meetings and feedback
> 5. Track daily writing progress with the Writing Sprint Tracker
> 6. Monitor deadlines with the Countdown section
>
> **Tip:** Writing a thesis is a marathon, not a sprint. Set small daily goals (300-500 words) rather than overwhelming weekend sessions. This planner helps you stay accountable to those small, consistent efforts.
