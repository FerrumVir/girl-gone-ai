# AI Business Prompts Pack — 105+ Prompts for Business Operations

---

## How to Use This Pack

This pack contains 105+ prompts organized across 9 business categories. Every prompt is copy-paste ready — replace the bracketed [PLACEHOLDER] variables with your specific business details before submitting to your AI assistant (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.).

**Tips for best results:**

1. **Replace every placeholder.** Generic inputs produce generic outputs. The more specific you are — naming competitors, providing real numbers, describing your actual customers — the more usable the output.
2. **Provide context first.** Before using a prompt, consider pasting a brief description of your business into the chat. This gives the AI background to work with.
3. **Chain prompts together.** Use a persona prompt first, then a marketing prompt that targets that persona. Each builds on the last.
4. **Treat outputs as first drafts.** AI gives you a strong starting point — add your judgment, insider knowledge, and nuance before using professionally.
5. **Save your best outputs.** When a prompt + your inputs produce great results, save the filled-in prompt as a template for future use.
6. **Iterate.** If the first result isn't right, add follow-ups like "Make the tone more authoritative" or "Add specific metrics and benchmarks."

**How placeholders work:**

- `[YOUR COMPANY]` — your company or brand name
- `[YOUR PRODUCT/SERVICE]` — what you sell
- `[INDUSTRY]` — your market vertical
- `[TARGET AUDIENCE]` — who you serve (be specific)
- `[COMPETITOR 1/2/3]` — named competitors
- `[TONE]` — the voice you want (e.g., "professional but approachable," "authoritative and data-driven")
- `[STAGE]` — your business stage (pre-launch, early, growth, established)
