# SECTION 1: WORKFLOW — FROM PROMPT TO PRODUCT

---

## The AI Art Business Workflow

```
STEP 1: Create → Generate high-quality images using tested prompts
STEP 2: Curate → Select only commercial-grade outputs
STEP 3: Refine → Upscale, edit, and prepare for delivery format
STEP 4: Package → Format for the specific sales channel
STEP 5: Price → Set prices based on usage rights and format
STEP 6: Publish → List on marketplace or deliver to client
STEP 7: Market → Drive traffic and build audience
```

---

## Quality Control Checklist (Before ANY image goes to market)

For each image, confirm:

- [ ] No obvious AI artifacts (extra fingers, melted text, warped edges)
- [ ] Composition is intentional and balanced
- [ ] Color palette is cohesive and appealing
- [ ] Resolution meets delivery requirements (minimum 300 DPI for print)
- [ ] No copyrighted characters, logos, or recognizable faces
- [ ] Style is consistent with your brand/portfolio
- [ ] Image tells a story or evokes a clear emotion
- [ ] Would I be proud to put my name on this? Y / N

**If any answer is NO — do not publish. Regenerate or refine.**

```
- Resale in any form
- Use in commercial products or marketing
- Modification for commercial purposes

```
