# Morning Reset Checklist Pack
### Five Routines for Five Energy Levels -- Print, Laminate, Reuse

---

> **HOW TO USE THIS PACK**
>
> 1. Print all five checklists (they're designed for standard letter / A4 paper)
> 2. Laminate them or slip them into sheet protectors
> 3. Each morning, pick the checklist that matches your energy
> 4. Use a dry-erase marker to check off each step
> 5. Wipe clean and reuse tomorrow
>
> There is no "right" checklist. High-energy mornings get The Full Reset. Rough mornings get The Gentle Reset. The only wrong choice is skipping your morning entirely.

---

---

# CHECKLIST 1: THE FULL RESET (20 Minutes)

> For mornings when you woke up with energy and want to start the day strong.

---

## Body (5 minutes)

- [ ] Drink a full glass of room-temperature water
- [ ] 5 deep breaths: inhale for 4 counts, hold for 4, exhale for 6
- [ ] Stretch: reach arms overhead, twist side to side, forward fold, touch toes
- [ ] Splash cold water on your face or do a 30-second cold rinse in the shower
- [ ] Take any morning vitamins or supplements

## Mind (5 minutes)

- [ ] Journal: write 3 things you are grateful for today
- [ ] Set your intention for the day in one sentence:
```
Today I will: _______________________________________________
```
- [ ] Read or listen to one page of something that inspires you (book, podcast, devotional)
- [ ] Say one affirmation out loud (even if it feels weird):
```
"I am capable, I am prepared, and today is mine."
```

## Plan (5 minutes)

- [ ] Review your calendar for today -- know what is coming
- [ ] Write your Top 3 priorities (if only three things get done, make it these):

| # | Priority | Why It Matters |
|---|----------|---------------|
| 1 | | |
| 2 | | |
| 3 | | |

- [ ] Identify your ONE non-negotiable task (must happen no matter what): ________________
- [ ] Scan email/messages for urgent items ONLY (set a 2-minute timer)

## Space (3 minutes)

- [ ] Make your bed (30 seconds of calm control)
- [ ] Tidy one surface (kitchen counter, desk, or nightstand)
- [ ] Fill your water bottle for the day

## Appearance (2 minutes)

- [ ] Get dressed intentionally (not the first thing you grab -- something that makes you feel capable)
- [ ] Quick skincare: cleanse, moisturize, SPF
- [ ] One finishing touch: perfume, earrings, lip balm, hair clip -- anything that signals "I showed up today"

# CHECKLIST 2: THE QUICK RESET (10 Minutes)

> For mornings when time is tight but you still want to start with intention.

---

## Essentials Only (10 minutes total)

- [ ] Drink a full glass of water (before coffee or anything else)
- [ ] 3 deep breaths (slow in, slow out -- takes 30 seconds)
- [ ] Write down your #1 priority for today:
```
The ONE thing that must get done today: _______________________________________________
```
- [ ] Quick face care: splash water, moisturizer, SPF
- [ ] Get dressed (pick something you feel good in -- it takes the same amount of time as grabbing sweats)
- [ ] Grab your bag, keys, water bottle
- [ ] One positive affirmation on your way out the door:
```
"I don't need a perfect morning. I just need a present one."
```
- [ ] Check calendar: what's the first thing I need to be ready for?
- [ ] Pack or plan lunch/snacks (even grabbing a bar counts)
- [ ] Phone on Do Not Disturb until you arrive where you're going

**Quick Reset Score:** ___/10 items checked

# CHECKLIST 3: THE GENTLE RESET (15 Minutes)

> For low-energy mornings when you need softness, not hustle.

---

## Permission to Go Slow

- [ ] Stay in bed for 2 extra minutes -- but keep your phone away
- [ ] Sit up slowly. Place your feet on the floor. Take 3 deep breaths before standing.
- [ ] Drink something warm: tea, coffee, lemon water, warm water with honey

## Gentle Body Care

- [ ] Wash your face with warm water (nothing harsh)
- [ ] Moisturize -- take an extra 30 seconds and actually feel it on your skin
- [ ] Put on something comfortable that still makes you feel human (not yesterday's clothes)
- [ ] Gentle stretch: roll your neck, shrug your shoulders, reach arms overhead
- [ ] Eat something small (even toast, a banana, or yogurt -- your body needs fuel)

## Gentle Mind Care

- [ ] Write one thing you are looking forward to today (even something tiny):
```
Today I am looking forward to: _______________________________________________
```
- [ ] Give yourself permission to not be "on" all day:
```
"Today is not about being perfect. It's about getting through."
```
- [ ] Identify the ONE thing you need to do today (just one):
```
If nothing else happens today, I need to: _______________________________________________
```
- [ ] Text or message one person something kind (a good morning, a thank you, an "I appreciate you")

## Gentle Environment

- [ ] Open a window or step outside for 60 seconds of fresh air
- [ ] Light a candle or turn on a lamp (not harsh overhead lights)
- [ ] Put on music, a podcast, or ambient sound that feels calming

**Gentle Reset Score:** ___/15 items checked

# CHECKLIST 4: THE WEEKEND RESET (Flexible -- No Timer)

> For slow mornings with no agenda. The only rule: no rushing.

---

## The No-Rush Morning

- [ ] No alarm -- wake up when your body wakes up
- [ ] Stay in bed and do nothing for as long as you want (no guilt)
- [ ] First beverage: make it special (actual brewed coffee, matcha, fresh juice -- not just grabbing a mug)

## Nourish

- [ ] Make your favorite breakfast (not just eat something -- actually cook/prepare something you love)
- [ ] Eat it sitting down, not standing at the counter
- [ ] Drink a full glass of water at some point this morning

## Brain Dump

Everything on your mind goes onto paper. Not organized, not prioritized. Just get it out of your head.

```
Brain Dump Space (write anything: tasks, worries, ideas, random thoughts):

____________________________________________
____________________________________________
____________________________________________
____________________________________________
____________________________________________
____________________________________________
____________________________________________
____________________________________________
```

## Weekend Intention

- [ ] Plan one fun or creative thing for today:
```
Today's fun thing: _______________________________________________
```
- [ ] Plan one restorative thing for today:
```
Today's rest thing: _______________________________________________
```
- [ ] Gratitude moment: name 3 things you would miss if they disappeared tomorrow

| # | I Am Grateful For |
|---|-------------------|
| 1 | |
| 2 | |
| 3 | |

## Optional Extras (No Pressure)

- [ ] Move your body in a way that feels good (walk, yoga, dance, stretch)
- [ ] Do one small organizing task (tidy a drawer, clear a shelf, organize your bag)
- [ ] Reach out to someone you have not talked to in a while
- [ ] Spend time outside -- even 10 minutes of sunlight changes your mood

**Weekend Reset Score:** ___/14 items checked (but honestly, no score needed -- it's the weekend)

# CHECKLIST 5: THE MONDAY RESET (15 Minutes)

> For starting the week with clarity, direction, and zero chaos.

---

## Prepare Your Mind (5 minutes)

- [ ] Review your calendar for the entire week (not just today)
- [ ] Write 3 goals for this week:

| # | Weekly Goal | Why It Matters |
|---|-----------|---------------|
| 1 | | |
| 2 | | |
| 3 | | |

- [ ] Identify the single most important task for today:
```
Today's #1 priority: _______________________________________________
```
- [ ] Anticipate one challenge this week and plan your response:

| Potential Challenge | My Plan |
|--------------------|---------|
| | |

## Prepare Your Space (5 minutes)

- [ ] Tidy your workspace (clear desk, organize papers, declutter)
- [ ] Prep your bag or work setup for the day
- [ ] Check that your phone is charged and you have what you need
- [ ] Fill water bottle
- [ ] Lay out or decide on outfit (if you haven't already)

## Prepare Your Energy (5 minutes)

- [ ] Eat a real breakfast (protein + something sustaining)
- [ ] Listen to one motivating podcast segment, song, or audiobook chapter on your commute or while getting ready
- [ ] Text someone something kind or encouraging -- start the week by giving energy, not just spending it
- [ ] Set one boundary for the week:
```
This week I will protect my time by: _______________________________________________
```
- [ ] Monday affirmation:
```
"This week does not happen to me. I happen to this week."
```

**Monday Reset Score:** ___/15 items checked
