# Plant Care & Garden Planner
## Indoor/Outdoor Plant Profiles, Watering Schedules, Seasonal Calendars & More

---

> **HOW TO USE THIS PLANNER**
>
> This digital planner is for plant parents, garden enthusiasts, and anyone who wants to keep their greenery thriving. Use it digitally or print the sections you need.
>
> 1. Start by adding your plants to the Plant Profiles section
> 2. Set up your Watering Schedule based on each plant's needs
> 3. Use the Seasonal Planting Calendar to plan what to plant and when
> 4. Design your garden layout in the Garden Bed section
> 5. Track harvests, propagation, and plant health over time
>
> **Tip:** Take a photo of each plant when you first get it and note the date. Comparing photos monthly is the best way to notice subtle changes in health or growth.

## Watering Guide by Category

| Category | Frequency (Summer) | Frequency (Winter) | Signs of Underwatering | Signs of Overwatering |
|----------|-------------------|-------------------|----------------------|---------------------|
| Tropicals | Every 5-7 days | Every 10-14 days | Drooping, crispy edges | Yellow leaves, mushy stems |
| Succulents | Every 10-14 days | Every 3-4 weeks | Wrinkled leaves, soft | Translucent leaves, rot |
| Ferns | Every 3-5 days | Every 7-10 days | Crispy fronds, dropping | Yellow fronds, root rot |
| Herbs (indoor) | Every 3-5 days | Every 5-7 days | Wilting, dry soil | Yellowing, fungus gnats |
| Vegetables | Daily-every 2 days | N/A (seasonal) | Wilting, flower drop | Splitting, root rot |
| Established Trees | Weekly deep soak | Monthly (if dry) | Leaf curl, premature drop | Fungal issues |

## Common Issues Quick Reference

| Symptom | Likely Cause | Solution |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| Yellow leaves (lower) | Overwatering or nitrogen deficiency | Reduce water / fertilize |
| Yellow leaves (all over) | Root rot, poor drainage | Repot, improve drainage |
| Brown leaf tips | Low humidity or salt buildup | Increase humidity / flush soil |
| Crispy leaf edges | Underwatering or too much direct sun | Water more / relocate |
| Leggy/stretching growth | Insufficient light | Move to brighter spot |
| White powdery coating | Powdery mildew | Improve airflow, neem oil |
| Tiny flying insects | Fungus gnats | Let soil dry, sticky traps |
| Sticky residue on leaves | Aphids or scale | Neem oil, insecticidal soap |
| Webbing on leaves | Spider mites | Increase humidity, neem spray |
| Mushy/soft stem base | Root rot / overwatering | Cut above rot, propagate |
| No new growth | Dormancy, root bound, or lacking nutrients | Assess season, check roots |
| Flower drop | Temperature stress or inconsistent watering | Stabilize environment |
