# Digital Declutter Challenge Kit
### 14 Days to a Clean, Organized, Stress-Free Digital Life

---

> **HOW THIS WORKS**
>
> One task per day. Each takes 15-45 minutes. Do them in order.
> By day 14, your digital life will be unrecognizable (in the best way).
>
> Rules:
> - One day at a time. Don't skip ahead.
> - Done is better than perfect. A partial cleanup beats no cleanup.
> - If a day takes longer than expected, split it across two days.
> - Check the box when complete. That dopamine hit is earned.

# DAY 1: EMAIL UNSUBSCRIBE AUDIT

**Time needed:** 30-45 minutes
**Goal:** Cut your daily email volume by 50%+

---

## Step 1: The Mass Unsubscribe Sweep

Open your email. Search for "unsubscribe" to find all newsletter/marketing emails.

**Unsubscribe Decision Filter — for EACH email, ask:**
- Did I open the last 3 emails from this sender? Y / N
- Would I re-subscribe today if I weren't already on the list? Y / N

# DAY 2: INBOX ZERO SYSTEM SETUP

**Time needed:** 30-45 minutes
**Goal:** Process every email in your inbox and set up a system to keep it clean

---

## Step 1: Create Your Folder Structure

Set up these folders/labels:

- [ ] ACTION NEEDED (emails requiring a response or task)
- [ ] WAITING FOR (emails where you're waiting on someone else)
- [ ] REFERENCE (important info you might need later)
- [ ] RECEIPTS (purchase confirmations, subscriptions)
- [ ] READ LATER (newsletters/articles you actually want to read)

## Step 2: Process Your Inbox

For EVERY email currently in your inbox, choose one action:

| Action | When to use it | Time limit |
|--------|---------------|------------|
| DELETE/ARCHIVE | You'll never need it again | 2 seconds |
| REPLY NOW | Takes under 2 minutes to respond | 2 minutes max |
| MOVE TO ACTION | Needs a real response/task | Move it |
| MOVE TO WAITING | Ball is in someone else's court | Move it |
| MOVE TO REFERENCE | Important info, no action needed | Move it |

## Step 3: Daily Email Routine (set this up now)

```
MORNING (5 min):
- [ ] Scan inbox for urgent items only
- [ ] Reply to anything under 2 min
- [ ] Move everything else to appropriate folder

MIDDAY (10 min):
- [ ] Process ACTION NEEDED folder
- [ ] Follow up on WAITING FOR items if overdue
- [ ] Unsubscribe from any new junk that arrived

END OF DAY (5 min):
- [ ] Inbox at zero (move/archive everything)
- [ ] Check ACTION NEEDED — anything for tomorrow?
- [ ] Done.
```

- [ ] DAY 2 COMPLETE
