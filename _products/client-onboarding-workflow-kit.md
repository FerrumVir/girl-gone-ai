# Client Onboarding Workflow Kit
### Everything You Need to Take a Client from "Yes!" to Active Project in 48 Hours

---

> **HOW TO USE THIS KIT**
>
> 1. Customize each template with your brand name, colors, and services
> 2. Follow the workflow in order for each new client
> 3. Copy/paste email templates directly — edit the [BRACKETS] with client details
> 4. Use the checklists to make sure nothing falls through the cracks
>
> Estimated setup time: 60–90 minutes (one time). Onboarding time per client after that: under 2 hours.

## Kickoff Call Agenda

**Date:** [DATE]

### Pre-Call Checklist (for you)

- [ ] Client questionnaire received and reviewed
- [ ] Any follow-up questions noted below

---

### Agenda

**1. Welcome & Rapport (5 min)**
- Thank them for choosing you
- Confirm they received all onboarding materials
- Quick personal check-in

**2. Questionnaire Review (10 min)**
- Confirm key details from their answers
- Ask follow-up questions:
  - Q: [QUESTION BASED ON THEIR ANSWERS]
  - Q: [QUESTION BASED ON THEIR ANSWERS]
  - Q: [QUESTION BASED ON THEIR ANSWERS]
- Clarify any vague answers

**3. Project Scope Confirmation (10 min)**
- Walk through deliverables:
  - Deliverable 1: [WHAT]
  - Deliverable 2: [WHAT]
  - Deliverable 3: [WHAT]
- Confirm what's included vs. out of scope
- Discuss revision policy: [# OF REVISIONS INCLUDED]
- Confirm budget and payment schedule

**4. Timeline & Milestones (10 min)**
- Share timeline:
  - Milestone 1: [WHAT] — Due [DATE]
  - Milestone 2: [WHAT] — Due [DATE]
  - Milestone 3: [WHAT] — Due [DATE]
  - Final delivery: [DATE]
- Confirm their availability for feedback windows
- Set next check-in date: [DATE]

**5. Files & Access Needed (5 min)**
- Review file request checklist together
- Confirm they know where to upload
- Set deadline for all files: [DATE]

**6. Questions & Wrap-Up (5 min)**
- "What questions do you have for me?"
- Confirm next steps for BOTH parties
- End on a high note — express excitement about the project

# TEMPLATE 7: COMPLETE ONBOARDING WORKFLOW CHECKLIST

---

## Master Onboarding Checklist

> Use this for EVERY new client. Check off each step as you complete it.

### Day 0: Contract Signed

- [ ] Contract/agreement signed by both parties
- [ ] Invoice sent (deposit or full payment)
- [ ] Payment received and confirmed
- [ ] Client added to your CRM/tracking system
- [ ] Project folder created (Google Drive/Dropbox)
- [ ] Project added to your calendar with all key dates

### Day 1: Welcome & Intake

- [ ] Welcome email sent (Template 1)
- [ ] Client questionnaire sent (Template 2)
- [ ] File request checklist sent (Template 4)
- [ ] Shared folder link sent to client
- [ ] Kickoff call scheduled (within 3-5 business days)
- [ ] Client added to project management tool
- [ ] Internal project brief started

### Day 2-3: Review & Prep

- [ ] Questionnaire responses received and reviewed
- [ ] Follow-up questions noted for kickoff call
- [ ] Files/assets received (or reminders sent)
- [ ] Timeline drafted (Template 5)
- [ ] Kickoff call agenda prepared (Template 3)
- [ ] Any necessary research completed

### Day 3-5: Kickoff

- [ ] Kickoff call completed
- [ ] Call recap email sent within 24 hours
- [ ] Timeline confirmed and shared with client
- [ ] Communication preferences confirmed
- [ ] All outstanding files received (or deadline extended)
- [ ] Project officially moved to "Active" status

### Ongoing: During Project

- [ ] Regular check-ins at agreed frequency
- [ ] Status updates sent at each milestone
- [ ] Feedback received and implemented per revision policy
- [ ] Scope changes documented and approved (if any)
- [ ] Timeline adjustments communicated promptly

### Project End: Offboarding

- [ ] Final deliverables sent with clear file naming
- [ ] All source files delivered (if included in scope)
- [ ] Implementation support provided (if included)
- [ ] Final invoice sent (if balance due)
- [ ] Final payment received
- [ ] Feedback form sent (Template 6) — wait 3-5 days after delivery
- [ ] Testimonial requested (if feedback is positive)
- [ ] Thank-you note or small gift sent
- [ ] Project archived in your system
- [ ] Follow-up reminder set for 30/60/90 days (for repeat business)
