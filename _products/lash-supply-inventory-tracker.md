# Lash Supply Inventory Tracker
### Never Run Out Mid-Appointment Again — Complete Supply Management System

---

> **HOW TO USE THIS TRACKER**
>
> 1. Fill in your current inventory (takes 30 minutes the first time)
> 2. Set reorder points for each item (minimum stock before you reorder)
> 3. Update quantities weekly (takes 5 minutes)
> 4. Review costs monthly to track spending and find savings
>
> When any item hits its reorder point: ORDER IMMEDIATELY. Don't wait.

### Waste Reduction Strategies

- [ ] Buy smaller adhesive bottles (use within 4-6 weeks of opening)
- [ ] Track open dates on ALL products with expiration concerns

- [ ] Store adhesive properly (airtight, dark, 65-75F)
- [ ] Don't decant too many lashes at once (prevent dust/humidity damage)
