## Which Beauty Business Model Fits You?

| Factor | Mobile/On-Location | Salon Suite/Booth Rental | Home Studio | Commission Salon | Virtual/Digital |
|--------|-------------------|--------------------------|-------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Startup cost** | $500-$2,000 | $2,000-$10,000 | $1,000-$5,000 | $0-$500 | $100-$1,000 |
| **Monthly overhead** | Low ($100-$300) | Medium ($800-$2,500) | Low ($200-$500) | $0 (they take %) | Very low ($50-$200) |
| **Client capacity** | 3-5/day | 5-8/day | 4-6/day | Set by salon | Unlimited (digital) |
| **Flexibility** | High — you set hours | Medium — set by lease | High — your rules | Low — salon hours | Highest |
| **Income potential** | $3K-$8K/mo | $5K-$15K/mo | $4K-$10K/mo | $2K-$5K/mo | $1K-$20K+/mo |
| **Licensing needs** | Full cosmetology/esthetics | Same + business license | Same + home permit | Varies | May not need one |
| **Client experience** | Convenient for them | Professional space | Intimate/personal | Salon atmosphere | Remote |
| **Scaling** | Limited by your body | Add services/hours | Limited by space | Limited by chair | Unlimited |
| **Best for** | Starting out, testing demand | Ready to commit, want own space | Introverts, low overhead | Learning, building clientele | Teaching, selling products |

## Quick Legit-Check: Am I Running a Real Business?

- [ ] Business bank account (separate from personal)
- [ ] Payment processing set up (not just Venmo/Cash App)
- [ ] Service prices publicly listed somewhere
- [ ] Booking system in place (not just DMs and texts)
- [ ] Cancellation policy written and shared with clients
- [ ] Client consent/intake forms used for every new client
- [ ] Income and expenses tracked weekly
- [ ] Insurance obtained (liability at minimum)
- [ ] Proper licensing for your state/city
- [ ] Tax money set aside (25-30% of profit)

**If you checked all 10:** You have a real business.
**Missing some:** Focus on those THIS WEEK. Each one protects you and makes you more professional.
