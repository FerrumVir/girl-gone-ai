### Hashtag Rules

1. Never use more than 20 hashtags on Instagram (algorithm penalizes spam)
2. Mix sizes: 2 large (500K+), 3 medium (50K-500K), 3 small (5K-50K), 2 micro (under 5K)
3. Always include 1-2 location hashtags
4. Rotate sets -- don't use the same exact hashtags on every post
5. Put hashtags in the caption, not the comments (algorithm now prefers this)
6. On TikTok: use 3-5 hashtags maximum, keep them short and trending

## QUICK-START GUIDE

### Step 1: Pick Your Niche Hashtags
Fill in the bracketed sections of the Hashtag Sets with your specific service, city, and niche terms. Save these as text replacements on your phone for fast pasting.

### Step 2: Batch Your Content
Set aside 2-3 hours one day per week to film 5-7 videos. Use the 30-Day Calendar as your shot list.

### Step 3: Write Captions in Advance
Pick 5 caption templates per week and fill them in with your specific details. Save them in your Notes app, ready to paste when you post.

### Step 4: Follow the Pillar Ratio
For every 10 posts: 3 transformations, 2-3 educational, 2 social proof, 1-2 personality, 1 sales. This keeps your feed balanced and avoids looking too salesy.

### Step 5: Track What Works
After 30 days, review your analytics. Note which hooks, captions, and content types got the most saves, shares, and profile visits. Double down on those formats.
