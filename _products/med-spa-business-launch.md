# SECTION 1: LICENSING & COMPLIANCE CHECKLIST

---

## Business Formation

- [ ] Choose business structure (LLC, PLLC, S-Corp, C-Corp)
- [ ] Register business name with Secretary of State
- [ ] Obtain EIN from IRS
- [ ] Open business bank account
- [ ] Hire business attorney (med spa specific experience preferred)
- [ ] Hire accountant/CPA familiar with medical practices
- [ ] Obtain business insurance (general liability)
- [ ] Obtain professional liability / malpractice insurance
- [ ] Workers' compensation insurance (required once you hire)
- [ ] Property/casualty insurance for equipment

## Medical Licensing (State-Specific — Verify ALL with your state board)

- [ ] Confirm Medical Director requirement for your state
  - Who can serve: MD, DO, NP, PA (varies by state)
  - Must they be on-site? (varies by state)
  - Required supervisory agreements in place
- [ ] Medical Director agreement signed
  - [ ] Scope of oversight defined
  - [ ] Compensation structure agreed
  - [ ] Availability/accessibility requirements met
- [ ] Facility license / permit obtained from state health department
- [ ] DEA registration (if offering controlled substances)
- [ ] CLIA waiver or certificate (if performing lab tests)
- [ ] Controlled substance license (state level, if applicable)
- [ ] Verify all practitioners' licenses current and in good standing:
  - [ ] Provider 1: [NAME] — License #: [NUMBER] — Expires: [DATE]
  - [ ] Provider 2: [NAME] — License #: [NUMBER] — Expires: [DATE]
  - [ ] Provider 3: [NAME] — License #: [NUMBER] — Expires: [DATE]

## Practice Compliance

- [ ] HIPAA compliance program established
  - [ ] Privacy policies written
  - [ ] Patient consent forms created
  - [ ] Staff HIPAA training completed
  - [ ] Business Associate Agreements with vendors
  - [ ] Breach notification plan documented
- [ ] OSHA compliance
  - [ ] Bloodborne pathogen exposure plan
  - [ ] Hazard communication plan
  - [ ] Sharps disposal protocol
  - [ ] Staff safety training
- [ ] Medical waste disposal contract signed
- [ ] Standard operating procedures (SOPs) written for all treatments
- [ ] Informed consent forms for each treatment
- [ ] Before/after photo consent forms
- [ ] Client intake forms (medical history, contraindications)
- [ ] Adverse event reporting protocol established
- [ ] Emergency protocol documented and posted
- [ ] AED on premises (recommended)
- [ ] First aid supplies stocked

## Zoning & Local Permits

- [ ] Confirm location zoned for medical/spa use
- [ ] Business operating permit/license from city/county
- [ ] Building permit (if renovating)
- [ ] Certificate of Occupancy
- [ ] Fire department inspection/approval
- [ ] Health department inspection (if required by state)
- [ ] Signage permit (exterior sign)
- [ ] Parking requirements met
