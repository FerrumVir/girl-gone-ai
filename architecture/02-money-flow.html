02 — Money Flow Diagram

Revenue through reinvestment — every gate and actor

About this diagram

This diagram traces every dollar from the moment it enters the system (Gumroad checkout) through cost deduction, the 15/85 owner-reinvestment split, and the two reinvestment gates that determine whether profits stay in the current project or seed the next one.

  • 15 / 85 rule: After all operating costs are deducted, 15% of net profit goes to the owner (Kenny) as a distribution. The remaining 85% stays in the reinvestment pool.
  • Seed-capital payback gate: No new project is seeded until the original $25k seed capital is fully repaid from the reinvestment pool.
  • ROI threshold gate: Even after payback, the current project must sustain at least 25% ROI before the Investor Agent is authorized to allocate funds to Project N+1.
  • Human approval checkpoints: Three actions always require explicit board/owner sign-off: initial seed deployment, hiring financial agents (Banker, Investor), and any ad-spend budget commitment.
  • Future revenue streams (ad revenue, subscriptions) are shown with dashed borders. They plug into the same Revenue node once activated.
