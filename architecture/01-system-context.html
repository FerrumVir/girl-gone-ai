01 — System Context Map

The recursive factory at the highest level

About this diagram

This System Context Map shows the NovoLink / Girl Gone AI recursive agent factory at its highest level of abstraction. Three layers are visible: the humans who govern and create content, the AI agents that operate within the Paperclip Agent Platform, and the external services that handle payments, hosting, video generation, and distribution.

  • Kenny Novotny sits at the top of the chain as Owner/Board. He directs and approves all major decisions through the CEO Agent.
  • CEO Agent is the central orchestrator, delegating to specialized agents: CMO (marketing), Founding Engineer (CTO, engineering), and planned Banker and Investor agents.
  • Jaci Ellis is the human influencer and COO assistant. She receives content briefs and tasks from the CMO Agent, then publishes and fulfills through Gumroad and social channels.
  • TJ provides technical guidance to the Founding Engineer agent as the human CTO.
  • Founding Engineer manages all technical integrations: deploying to GitHub Pages, building dashboards on Bubble.io, generating avatar videos with HeyGen, and connecting to Gumroad via API.
  • Planned agents (dashed borders) include the Banker Agent for finance, the Investor Agent for investment allocation, and G2 -- a per-project agent that enables recursion by seeding Project N+1.
  • The entire agent layer runs inside the Paperclip Agent Platform, shown as the dashed purple boundary.
Banker Agent, Investor Agent, and G2 are planned but not yet active. Their connections represent intended architecture, not current state.
