# Content Creator Editorial Calendar
### A Notion Template for Consistent, Multi-Platform Content

---

> 1. Duplicate this template to your Notion workspace using the link provided

## Template Overview

This Notion workspace replaces the sticky notes, scattered Google Docs, and color-coded calendar hacks you have been using to manage content. It gives you one connected system for ideation, production, scheduling, publishing, analytics, and repurposing -- across every platform you publish on.

Built for creators who are serious about growth and tired of winging it.

---

## What's Included

### 1. Content Master Database

The core of the system. Every content piece across every platform lives here.

**Properties:**
- Title, Platform (select), Content Type (video, post, article, newsletter, etc.)
- Status (Idea, Scripting, In Production, Review, Scheduled, Published, Archived)
- Publish Date, Deadline
- Content Pillar (relation to your strategic pillars)
- Assigned To (for teams)
- SEO Keywords, Hashtags
- Overdue Flag (formula -- auto-flags anything past deadline that isn't published)
- Performance Score (linked from Analytics Log)

**Views:**
- Calendar View -- visual monthly publishing schedule
- Kanban Pipeline -- grouped by production status
- This Week -- filtered to current week's content
- This Month -- filtered to current month
- By Platform -- one filtered view per platform
- Overdue -- anything past deadline and not yet published
- Best Performers -- sorted by views/engagement

### 2. Platform Tracker

Strategic planning for each platform you publish on.

**Properties:**
- Platform Name, URL/Handle
- Posting Frequency Goal (e.g., 3x/week)
- Best Posting Times
- Content Pillars for this platform
- Audience Notes
- Platform-Specific Goals (follower target, engagement rate target)
- Current Metrics Snapshot

### 3. Content Idea Bank

A dedicated space for capturing ideas without cluttering your active pipeline.

**Properties:**
- Idea Title, Description/Notes
- Source (where the idea came from)
- Platform Fit (which platforms could this work on)
- Urgency (Timely, Evergreen, Seasonal)
- Readiness (Raw Idea, Needs Research, Ready to Develop)
- Promoted to Content (checkbox -- moves it to the Master Database)

### 4. Repurposing Tracker

Map how your best content multiplies across platforms.

**Properties:**
- Original Content (relation to Master Database)
- Derivative Content (relation to Master Database)
- Repurpose Type (e.g., Blog to Newsletter, YouTube to Reels, Thread to Post)
- Status (Not Started, In Progress, Published)
- Untapped Derivatives (formula -- counts how many planned repurposes haven't been done)

### 5. Analytics Log

Track performance data to understand what actually works.

**Properties:**
- Content Piece (relation to Master Database)
- Date Pulled, Platform
- Views, Likes, Comments, Shares, Saves
- Engagement Rate (formula)
- Link Clicks, Conversions
- Revenue Attributed
- Notes/Insights

**Views:**
- By Content Piece
- By Platform
- Top Performers (sorted by views)
- Highest Engagement Rate
- Monthly Summary

## Planning Templates

### Weekly Planning Template
A fill-in page for your weekly planning session (15-20 minutes):
- Review last week's published content and performance
- Set this week's publishing targets per platform
- Assign content pieces to specific days
- Identify any pieces that need repurposing
- Note any timely/trending topics to jump on

### Monthly Planning Template
A deeper review and forward-planning session (30-45 minutes):
- Monthly content performance review
- Top 3 performers and why they worked
- Content pillar balance check
- Next month's content themes
- Repurposing backlog review
- Platform growth check against goals

## Production Checklists

Built directly into the template as toggle blocks:

1. **Pre-Production** -- Research, outline, script, assets gathered, thumbnail planned
2. **Post-Production** -- Edited, captioned, thumbnail created, copy written, hashtags added
3. **Post-Publish** -- Shared to stories, cross-posted, engaged with comments for first hour
4. **30-Day Analytics Pull** -- Pull metrics, log to Analytics database, note insights

## Tips for Getting the Most Out of This Template

- **Do a weekly planning session.** 15 minutes on Sunday or Monday. It changes everything.
- **Use the Idea Bank aggressively.** Capture every idea the moment it hits. Most won't become content, but the ones that do will be better for having been captured.
- **Check the Repurposing Tracker monthly.** Your best content from 2 months ago probably has 4-5 derivatives you never created.
- **Log analytics consistently.** Even rough numbers once a month. The patterns only emerge with data over time.
- **Start with one platform view.** Don't try to manage all platforms at once in the beginning. Focus on your primary platform first, then expand.
- **Use the Overdue flag.** If something is flagged, either publish it, reschedule it, or archive it. Don't let the backlog grow.
