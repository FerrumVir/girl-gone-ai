# Wellness & Habit Tracker
### A Notion Template for Your Health, Habits, and Wellbeing

---

> **QUICK START -- Read This First (Takes 3 Minutes)**
>
> 1. Duplicate this template to your Notion workspace using the link provided
> 2. Open **Today's Daily Wellness Log** and fill in the morning section (2 minutes)
> 3. Pick 2-3 habits in the **Habit Tracker** to start with -- no more than 3
> 4. At the end of your day, come back and fill in the evening section
> 5. Repeat tomorrow. After 7 days, do your first Weekly Review.
>
> Don't try to use every section on day one. Start with the daily log and habits. Add the rest over time.

---

## Template Overview

This Notion workspace brings your health, habits, mood, exercise, meals, and self-care into one connected system. Instead of juggling five different apps that can't talk to each other, everything lives in one place -- so after 30 days, your own data shows you patterns you have never been able to see before.

No medical expertise required. If you can fill in a form in Notion, you can use this template.

---

## What's Included

### 1. Daily Wellness Log

A structured daily entry that takes 3-5 minutes morning and evening.

**Morning Check-In:**
- Hours of sleep, sleep quality (1-5)
- Morning mood (1-5 scale with optional notes)
- Anxiety level, stress level (1-5)
- Intentions for the day
- Gratitude entry (3 things)

**Evening Check-In:**
- Water intake (glasses)
- Meals logged (brief notes or link to meal plan)
- Exercise done (yes/no, link to Exercise Log)
- Evening mood (1-5)
- Energy level throughout the day
- Win of the day
- Optional journal prompt (6 rotating prompts provided)

### 2. Habit Tracker

A fully customizable habit tracking system.

**Properties per Habit:**
- Habit Name, Category (Health, Fitness, Mental, Productivity, Creative, Social)
- Frequency Target (daily, 3x/week, weekdays, custom)
- Cue/Trigger (what reminds you to do it)
- Why This Matters (your personal reason)
- Difficulty Rating (1-5)
- Current Streak, Best Streak

**Views:**
- Weekly Grid -- check off each day
- Monthly Overview -- see completion patterns at a glance
- By Category -- group habits by life area
- Struggling Habits -- filtered to lowest completion rates

**Weekly Reflection Prompts:**
- What made habits easy this week?
- What made them hard?
- What do I want to adjust for next week?

### 3. Exercise Log Database

Log every workout with meaningful detail.

**Properties:**
- Date, Type (Cardio, Strength, Yoga, Walking, Sports, etc.)
- Duration, Distance (optional)
- Intensity (1-5 scale)
- Pre-Workout Feeling, Post-Workout Feeling
- Personal Records (PRs)
- Notes

**Views:**
- All Workouts (chronological)
- By Type
- Monthly Calendar View
- This Month's Stats

### 4. Meal Planning System

Plan your week and build a grocery list without nightly decision fatigue.

**Includes:**
- Weekly Meal Plan Grid (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks for 7 days)
- Grocery List (auto-organized by category: produce, protein, dairy, pantry, frozen)
- Recipe Database with tags, ratings, prep time, nutritional notes, and instructions

### 5. Mental Health Check-In

A weekly mood and mental health tracker.

**Weekly Properties:**
- Average Morning Mood, Average Evening Mood
- Anxiety and Stress Patterns
- What drained you this week
- What restored you this week
- What you want more of next week
- Gratitude log

### 6. Self-Care Menu

A curated list of self-care activities organized by time available:
- **5 minutes:** Deep breathing, stretching, step outside, drink water mindfully
- **15 minutes:** Short walk, journaling, guided meditation, call a friend
- **30 minutes:** Workout, bath, cook a healthy meal, read
- **1 hour:** Long walk, yoga class, creative project, full meal prep

When you are depleted and cannot think, just pick one thing from the menu.

### 7. Monthly Wellness Review

A data-driven monthly review template that pulls your numbers together:
- Average sleep hours and quality
- Average mood scores (morning and evening)
- Total exercise days and types
- Hydration average
- Habit completion rates
- Correlation prompts: What happens to your mood when you sleep 7+ hours? What does exercise do to your energy? What patterns emerge around anxiety?

### 8. Health Goal Database

Set 1-3 health goals with structure.

**Properties:**
- Goal Name, Category, Target, Timeline
- Why This Matters (personal motivation)
- Linked Habits (relation to Habit Tracker)
- Milestones (sub-goals)
- Weekly Check-In Log
- Obstacle Planning (what might get in the way and how to handle it)

### 9. Supplement & Medication Tracker

A daily log for supplements and medications.

**Properties:**
- Name, Dose, Timing (morning, afternoon, evening)
- Taken (checkbox)
- Notes (side effects, observations)

## Tips for Getting the Most Out of This Template

- **Start small.** Daily Wellness Log + 2-3 habits. That is enough for week one.
- **Be honest, not perfect.** A mood rating of 2 is just as valuable as a 5. The data is the point.
- **Do the Monthly Review.** This is where the real insights live. 30 days of data reveals patterns that day-to-day tracking cannot.
- **Use the Self-Care Menu on hard days.** Don't try to think when you are depleted. Just pick from the list.
- **Customize freely.** Add properties that matter to you, remove ones that don't. This is a starting structure, not a rigid prescription.
- **Missing a day is fine.** Pick back up tomorrow. Gaps in data are normal and expected.
