# Logo Concepts for 'Girl Gone AI' (90s Aesthetic)

1. **Neon Cyberpunk (Vaporwave)**
   - **Description:** A stylized silhouette of a woman's head merged with digital glitch artifacts. The color palette uses electric pink, cyan, and deep purple. The text "Girl Gone AI" is rendered in a glowing neon font with slight chromatic aberration.
   - **Prompt:** 90s vaporwave aesthetic logo, "Girl Gone AI" text, neon pink and cyan colors, glitch art effects, cyberpunk silhouette, high resolution, vector style, dark background.

2. **Retro Tech (8-Bit/Pixel Art)**
   - **Description:** A pixelated icon of a computer chip or a floppy disk that transforms into a feminine profile. Uses a limited color palette of bright green, grey, and black, reminiscent of early gaming consoles.
   - **Prompt:** 8-bit pixel art logo, "Girl Gone AI", retro computer tech theme, grid patterns, green and grey color scheme, 90s gaming aesthetic, clean pixel edges, high resolution.

3. **High-Octane 90s (Vibrant/Organic)**
   - **Description:** Dynamic, sweeping organic curves and motion lines that suggest energy. Bold gradients of orange, teal, and magenta. The typography is chunky and slightly italicized to imply speed.
   - **Prompt:** 90s high-octane aesthetic logo, "Girl Gone AI", vibrant gradients, teal and orange, organic sweeping curves, motion lines, energetic composition, pop culture style, high resolution.

4. **Geometric Pop (Memphis Style)**
   - **Description:** A collage of bold geometric shapes (squiggles, triangles, circles) in primary colors (yellow, blue, red) against a white or pastel background. The text is a clean, bold sans-serif.
   - **Prompt:** Memphis style logo, "Girl Gone AI", geometric shapes, squiggles and triangles, bright primary colors, 90s pop art aesthetic, bold composition, clean vector look, high resolution.

5. **Digital Revolution (Circuit/Tech)**
   - **Description:** A sleek, professional look featuring circuit board patterns forming a circular emblem. The colors are deep navy and cyan with bright neon green accents representing "data flow."
   - **Prompt:** Digital revolution logo, "Girl Gone AI", circuit board patterns, navy and cyan color palette, neon accents, tech-inspired, futuristic 90s, clean lines, high resolution.
