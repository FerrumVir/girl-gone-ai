How It Works

  1. Customer pays on Gumroad via credit card or PayPal. Gumroad handles all payment processing, fraud detection, and tax collection.
  2. Gumroad deducts fees: 10% on the Discover plan (no monthly fee) or 5% + $10/mo on Pro. This is the single largest variable cost.
  3. Bi-weekly payout to the NovoLink bank account. Funds clear 2-5 business days after payout initiation.
  4. Costs are deducted from the operating account: ad spend (variable), platform subscriptions (fixed), agent token costs (semi-fixed), human compensation (variable/fixed mix), and hosting (free via GitHub Pages).
  5. Net profit is computed monthly by the Banker Agent (me). If the seed capital ($25K) has been repaid AND rolling ROI >= 25%, the 15/85 split activates.
  6. 15% goes to Kenny as owner draw via monthly bank transfer. 85% flows to the reinvestment pool managed by the Investor Agent.
ASSUMPTION (needs validation): Gumroad Discover plan at 10% fee. If upgraded to Pro ($10/mo, 5% fee), breakeven at ~$200/mo revenue. Recommend Pro once monthly revenue exceeds $250.

Actors who touch money:

  • Gumroad — payment processing, fee deduction, payout
  • Bank (NovoLink account) — holds all operating funds
  • Banker Agent — calculates split, flags thresholds, produces reports
  • Investor Agent — decides reinvestment allocation from the 85% pool
  • CEO Agent — approves any spend >$1K, oversees all financial decisions
  • Kenny (Owner) — receives 15% draw, final authority on all financial matters

Approval gates:

  • Any single expenditure >$1,000 requires owner poll approval before execution
  • 15/85 split activation requires seed repayment + ROI gate verification
  • New project seeding requires Investor Agent proposal + CEO approval

Revenue Recognition Policy

  • Accrual basis (reporting): Revenue is recognized on the sale date — when the customer completes purchase on Gumroad. This is used for all P&L reporting and margin calculations.
  • Cash basis (cash flow): Cash is recognized when funds clear the bank account. Gumroad pays out bi-weekly, with a 2-5 day clearing window. This creates a ~2-3 week lag between sale and cash availability.

Gate Conditions for Split Activation

  1. Seed Repaid: Cumulative net profit >= $25,000 (initial seed capital)
  2. ROI >= 25%: (Cumulative Net Profit - Seed) / Seed >= 0.25, i.e., cumulative net profit >= $31,250
  3. Both gates must pass simultaneously for the 15/85 split to activate. Before that, 100% of net profit is reinvested in the current project.

50% Downside Scenario: At half projected revenue (~$807K Y1), seed repaid by Month 3-4, gates still pass by Month 4. Split activation delayed ~1 month. Flywheel still works.
